Our Vison

To create a world class Electrical Equipment Manufacturing and Distribution company that will stand the test of time and contribute to the infrastructural development of Nigeria.

Our Mission

To provide genuine and reasonable priced High and Medium voltage equipments in Nigeria and around the world in a one-stop shopping environment.

Management

We are managed by professionals with years of experience operating with high level of proficiency and our qualified staff ranks the best when it comes to effective services. Our team of highly trained professionals will give you expert services and support towards acquiring, installing and commissioning your electric transmission and distribution equipments. Our business is your business. Chief Ralph Eze-Okoli is the Chairman and Managing Director of the company. A graduate of Kaduna Polytechnic , Kaduna; he has been in business for over fourty (40) years and has been consistent with the Electrical materials/equipment Sub-sector. He has attended several courses on the Distributive Trade in Nigeria overseas. He moved the company’s headquarters from Zaria, Kaduna State to Lagos State in order to benefit from the economies of scale inherent in Lagos. He is an Executive Member of Alaba International Market Association, a Rotarian and a member of the Federal Road Safety Commission Special Marshal Unit 17, Alaba. He was the former President of Ukpor Improvement Union, Lagos branch (his town union), Chairman Elder’s Council, Industrial Market Association and Chairman Oba International Market Association Electrical Section. He is married with children.
